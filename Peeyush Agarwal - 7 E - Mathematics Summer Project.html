Project Guidelines

EFK activities for grade 7 Mathematics: Theme for April 2021 is “Earth — Environmental Engineering”.

  1. Integers (height of plant)
    Aim: To apply the concept of Integers to identify the total length of the plant (above the mud is positive and root is negative)
    Source to collect information: Google.
    Procedure:
    1. Consider any plant from the garden.
    2. Find the total length of the plant (above the mud is positive and root is negative)
    3. Compare Between the 5 lengths of plants.

    Observation Table:
    Sr. no. Plant Height Above Mud Height Below Mud Total Height


  2. Fractions:
    Students will find what fraction of area is covered by Maharashtra state with respect to area of India.
